Reviews

★★★★★

Susanne Sir’s Murder in the Shores: Six Hands is a delightfully whimsical, heartwarming, and suspense filled installment in the Georgi Girl series. It blends mystery, humor, and community spirit with effortless charm. Sir’s talent for creating lovable characters and lively adventure shines brightly in this engaging tale set in the sunny village of Miami Shores.

— Pacific Book Review
★★★★★

Murder in the Shores: Six Hands is a sharply constructed, atmospheric mystery that blends psychological tension with brisk, cinematic storytelling. With its vivid seaside setting and its cast of characters whose motives are layered, conflicted, and wholly human, the book succeeds as both a gripping whodunit and a thoughtful examination of how past wounds echo into the present.

— Clarion Reviews
★★★★★

A breezy, eccentric cozy mystery that blends community warmth, offbeat humor, and light supernatural touches into a distinctly Floridian whodunit. Set in the charming enclave of Miami Shores, the novel introduces an eclectic cast and unfolds with playful momentum and a communal spirit.

— Kirkus Reviews
